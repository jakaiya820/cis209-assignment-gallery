About Me

Hey, I’m Jakaiya Black. I’m currently studying Computer Information Systems and learning web development.

I’m really interested in cybersecurity and network security because I like understanding how systems are protected and how security works behind the scenes.

I chose Computer Information Systems because it was the closest path to Computer Science at my community college, and it felt like the right starting point for me.

Right now I’m building skills in HTML, CSS, and website development. After I finish at George Corley Wallace State Community College Selma, I plan to join the Air Force Reserve and later attend Tennessee State University for a double major in Computer Science and Communications.

My long-term goal is to work in a corporate tech role after completing my education and military plans.

Outside of tech, I enjoy drawing, painting, photography, music, poetry, and sports. Being creative actually helps me think better when solving problems.

If a recruiter looked at my site, I’d want them to think: creative, disciplined, and professional. This website is the project I’m most proud of so far because it shows my progress.
